PROJECT

Customer Reward Analysis For TVQ Supermart

View Project

Statement of Problem

Poor Sales Turnover prompted investigation into the Reward Process of TVQ Supermart A Process designed to improve sales and Customer Retention.

Brief Report

TVQ Supermart has a total of 5187 registered customers and only 2102 of the registered customers(40.51%) have received a reward(2% discount). 3087(50.49%) registered customers have never received a reward, which means, they have never purchased goods worth N10000 since the creation of their customer account.

The Customers were classified into 3 major groups

1. Those who have never redeemed reward - 3087(50.49%)

2. Those who have a reward but have never redeemed it - 531(10.23%).

3. Those who have rewards and have also redeemed rewards in the past - 1571(30.28%).

Presently 950 Customers have (2%) worth rewards available in their accounts 161 Customers have (4%) worth of reward available each, 56 Customers have (6%) worth of reward available each, 17 Customers have (8%) worth of reward available each, 16 Customers have (10%) worth of reward available each, 11 Customers have (12%) worth of reward available each, 6 Customers have (14%) worth rewards available each, 4 Customers have (16%) worth rewards available each, 3 Customers have (18%) worth rewards available each, 2 Customers have (20%) worth of reward available each, while 7 Customers have more than (22%) worth rewards available each.

Limitations of the Reward Process

1. The Cashier (Attendant) must constantly ask customers for account registration information in order to post purchases to their accounts.

2.Having a single point of payment becomes a limiting factor during rush hours.

3.Customers should always keep in mind to remind the cashier to post purchased goods to their account and there is a constant need to remind customers of the rewards of this process.

4.Customers having more than one reward can only claim one at a time

Recommendations

1.legible notice made visible and targeted at reminding customers of reward process

2.Cashier should always at all times request for customers account information and post purchases to account. Cashier should always inform customers with available reward to claim their rewards.

3.Customers should be allowed to claim more than one reward at the point of payment. .
